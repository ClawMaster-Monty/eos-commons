# Privacy Policy — EOS Commons

**Effective Date: July 5, 2026**

---

## Overview

EOS Commons ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## What We Collect

### Through the Peptide Calculator Bot
When you use the bot, we may log:
- **Discord username** — to identify who ran a calculation
- **Calculation inputs** — peptide type, dose requested, weight (if provided)
- **Calculation outputs** — results generated
- **Timestamp** — when the calculation was run

### Through Our Website / Landing Page
When you sign up for early access or the free tier:
- **Email address** — for onboarding and community access
- **Discord username** — to send you an invite link
- **Referral source** — how you found us (optional)

## How We Use Your Data

- **To deliver the calculation service** — calculating dosages and displaying results
- **To send community invites** — one email with a Discord invite link
- **To improve our tools** — aggregate analytics on what peptides are most calculated
- **To run the community** — member management, onboarding, support

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
- We do **not** collect health conditions, medical histories, or specific medical information
- We do **not** use your data for advertising or affiliate marketing
- We do **not** share your information with external services except those required to operate the community (Discord, Notion)

## Where Your Data Lives

All calculation logs are stored in a **private Notion database** accessible only to EOS Commons operators.

All member data is stored in **Notion** and is not accessible to the public.

## Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or third-party tracking on our landing page.

## Data Retention

- Calculation logs are retained for **12 months** and then deleted
- Email addresses are retained until a member requests deletion
- You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting EOS Commons

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** — request a copy of your data
- **Correction** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Object** — object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us via Discord.

## Data Security

We use reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact EOS Commons via Discord.
